About Snowbots

UBC Snowbots builds autonomous robots that navigate through complex terrain to reach GPS waypoints. This year, Snowbots became the first team to represent UBC in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition, a leading robot race hosting over 30 elite teams from all over the world including Turkey, India, and Japan.The robots are completely autonomous vehicles that are equipped with various sensors that can navigate to GPS waypoints dodging obstacles and following paths completely by themselyves. This multi-disciplinary feat calls for the integration of input from mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, and software programmers in order to design an effective vehicle that is capable of traversing the course both physically and on a software level.
