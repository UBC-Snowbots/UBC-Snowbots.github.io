About Snowbots

We are a student team dedicated to the research and development of autonomous ground vehicles.Our robots are equipped with various sensors which provides input about the environment, allowing it to navigate between set GPS waypoints in the shortest time possible, while avoid obstacles and staying within bounds of the course.

We are looking for ambitious students who wants to learn and contribute their skills in constructing an effective vehicle in the areas of mechanical, electrical, and software designs.

Our Robot

Our robot uses a LIDAR laser scanner for detecting physical objects and several webcams for detecting colored boundaries painted on the grounds. Internally, our camera vision system uses OpenCV to provide fast image processing and capturing techniques, which is essential for operating in performance critical situations.



Software

The software portion of our robot is primarily developed in C++, using the Robot Operating System (ROS) framework, with some specific portions written in Python and C.
